### Reflection

1.  What leads to my best learning: I learn best when I write the
    programs myself, test them with multiple values, and carefully read
    the instructions instead of rushing. When I debug my own mistakes
    instead of immediately looking for answers, I understand concepts
    better.

2.  Getting the most out of teaching: I am trying to actively watch
    lecture videos and follow along by writing code rather than just
    watching passively. I still need to improve consistency in reviewing
    material before practical sessions.

3.  Percentage of lecture videos watched: Approximately 85%.

4.  Time on task outside contact hours: Around 6–8 focused hours per
    week.

5.  One change to improve consistency: I will schedule fixed weekly
    revision sessions to revisit lecture concepts and re‑write selected
    practice problems without looking at solutions.
